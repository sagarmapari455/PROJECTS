Eligibility Criteria

At IST, we ensure that every student thrives in their academic journey. To cater to only the best-in-class students, we have set up some specific eligibility requirements to identify candidates who are ready to embrace the opportunity of a world-class technical education.

Educational Qualification

A minimum of 60% in 10th and 12th (PCM) from a recognized board

Entrance Exam

Must qualify for the Intellipaat National Eligibility Test (sst)

Qualify the Interview

Applicants must clear the 1:1 interview

Age Limit

Applicants must be 2023 / 2024 / 2025 / 2026 12th passout.
