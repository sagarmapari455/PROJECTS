What makes our Computer Science program unique?

Answer:--Our program focuses on practical, industry-oriented learning. Students work on real projects, hands-on coding labs and modern tools used by software companies. The curriculum is updated regularly to match the latest technology trends.

How do we prepare students for top tech careers?

Answer:--We train students through coding practice, aptitude sessions, interview preparation and soft-skills development. Regular workshops, hackathons and expert talks help students gain confidence for placements in leading IT companies.

What opportunities do students get during the course?

Answer:--Students get access to internships, industry visits, technical events, mini-projects and research activities. They also participate in competitions, seminars and collaborative projects that enhance innovation and teamwork.

Programs We Offer

B.Tech in
Computer Science & Engineering

This program focuses on core programming, data structures, algorithms, databases and operating systems. Students learn to design, develop and test software applications using modern tools and technologies.

B.Tech in
Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

This program covers machine learning, data analytics and AI-based problem solving. Students work with real datasets, build predictive models and explore the basics of neural networks and automation.

B.Tech in
Information Technology

The IT program concentrates on web development, networking, cloud computing and software project management. It prepares students to handle real-time applications, servers and enterprise-level systems.
